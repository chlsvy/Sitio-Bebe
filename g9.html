Our Mission

Our mission is to donate and give help and assistance to Sitio Bebe community for betterment. Furthermore, take initiatives to support an organization and invest in a better future and cause for their community. Donations may help Sitio Bebe in addressing the scarcity of water and the shortage of health assistance. Rainwater harvesting is one of the solutions that we can implement. Your contributions can assist them in purchasing the necessary equipments to address their issue. In summary, our team aim to help Sitio Bebe and strive for what's best to improve their communities and give them access to a new and better environment.

    Sitio Bebe

    The Community's Problem and Situation

    About Sitio Bebe

    Sitio Bebe is facing challenges with access to clean water and health professionals. Many people in Sitio bebe struggle to find safe drinking water, which leads to health problems, and there are not enough doctors and nurses in Sitio bebe, making it difficult for people to get the medical care they need. Their community is completely messed up through multiple damages and issues in their environment till then, conflicts started developing through these problems among the people.

    Water Shortage

    The city of Sitio Bebe is far from the source of water, so they lack water for their everyday lives, and one problem happened, and one of the reasons is the scarcity of water, which is their dry season and always hot weather. They get a poor supply of water through the usual weather changes in their community. Another reason for their poor supply of water is their pipes and sewage paths and the distance of their waterway.

    Scarcity of Health Professionals

    A further issue that they are dealing with also is the lack of health professionals. The reason Sitio Bebe lacks health professionals is the lack of college courses for those who want to be a health professional. Even if there are some, it's far from their city.

    Problems of Sitio Bebe

  • Water Supply
  • Pipes and Sewages
  • Lack of Health Professionals
  • Lack of Medical Needs/Equipments
